FAQ

What is SAMWAAD?

Commemorating the role of women in the fields of sciences, technology, engineering and mathematics and elevating their contribution in these domains by catering to their needs, providing them with opportunities and felicitating the meritorious, IEEE celebrates the eternal legacy of the women’s role in engineering by organizingan event called WIE. The paramount aim of WIE is exalting the role of women in engineering.

Who all are eligible to participate in the hackathon?

Any undergraduate student, irrespective of location in India, their college, branch, language or gender can participate.

Can only girls/women participate?

No, any student irrespective of the gender they identify themselves as, is allowed to participate. The problem statements are related to women, but anybody with an idea can participate.

What is the maximum number of participants in a team allowed?

A minimum of an individual and maximum of four members are allowed to be in a team.

How do we submit our ideas?

Submissions are required to be made on the Devfolio platform. The teams are encouraged to submit a video presenting a demo of what they have built along with the link to the GitHub repository and the hosted link (optional). Further submission guidelines can be found here - ideate.ieeenitb.com

. Is it necessary to host your solution?

The video demo and GitHub link are compulsorily required while making a submission. A hosted link to your solution is optional, however it'll give you an edge over other participants.
